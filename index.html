Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 28, 2026  ·  Nox Dev Team

Nox is built on a zero-knowledge architecture. We do not collect, store, transmit, or have access to any of your personal data. Ever.

What we collect

Nothing. Nox does not collect any personal data, usage data, analytics, or telemetry. There are no accounts, no sign-ups, and no data sent to any server operated by us.

How your data is stored

All vault data is stored exclusively on your device using iOS Keychain (iOS) or Android Keystore (Android), encrypted with AES-256. Your PIN is never stored — only a cryptographic hash is saved, which cannot be reversed to reveal your PIN.

Backup files are encrypted on your device before being saved to iCloud Drive or any other destination you choose. We have no access to these files.

Third-party services

Nox uses the following third-party service solely for processing in-app purchases:

  • RevenueCat — handles subscription and purchase state. RevenueCat may receive an anonymous app user ID and purchase receipt for validation purposes. No personal vault data is shared. See RevenueCat's privacy policy at revenuecat.com/privacy.

The app does not use analytics SDKs, advertising networks, tracking pixels, or any other third-party service that could access your data.

On-device OCR

Nox includes an optional document scanning feature powered by on-device OCR. Images are processed entirely on your device and deleted immediately after scanning. No image or extracted text is ever transmitted to any server.

Notifications

Expiry reminders and security tips are scheduled locally on your device using the operating system's notification APIs. No data is sent to external servers to deliver notifications.

Data recovery

Because we have no access to your data, we cannot recover your vault if you lose your device or forget your PIN without a recovery option set up. This is by design.

Children's privacy

Nox is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us through the App Store review or feedback channels.
